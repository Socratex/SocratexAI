# Project Pack: Personal

## Purpose

Use this pack for personal operations, life management, job search, capacity planning, habits, commitments, and decision support.

This pack should reduce chaos. It should not maximize task volume.

## Recommended Files

- `STATE.md`
- `AREAS.md`
- `TARGETS.md`
- `NEXT-ACTIONS.md`
- `BACKLOG.md`
- `DECISIONS.md`
- `RISKS.md`
- `REVIEW.md`
- `JOURNAL.md`

## Operating Defaults

- Prefer realistic capacity over optimistic planning.
- Separate must-do commitments from optional improvements.
- Track follow-ups explicitly.
- Keep next actions small and observable.
- Record decisions when they affect time, money, health, relationships, work, or identity.
- Treat avoidance, overload, ambiguity, and missing information as diagnosable constraints, not moral failures.

## Job Search Add-On

For job search projects, add:

- `APPLICATIONS.md`
- `TARGET-ROLES.md`
- `CV-VARIANTS.md`
- `INTERVIEW-PREP.md`
- `NETWORKING.md`

Track:

- company,
- role,
- source,
- status,
- next follow-up date,
- materials used,
- interview notes,
- rejection or acceptance signal,
- lessons learned.

## Review Gate

Check:

- Is the plan realistic for current capacity?
- Are open loops visible?
- Are follow-ups dated?
- Is the next action concrete?
- Are priorities based on expected leverage, not anxiety?
- Is the system adding clarity rather than administrative burden?
